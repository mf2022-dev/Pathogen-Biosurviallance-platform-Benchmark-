# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone contributing to the Pathogen Surveillance Evaluation Framework.

## Standards

- Be respectful and constructive
- Provide evidence-based evaluations
- Maintain objectivity in platform assessments
- Welcome diverse perspectives
- Focus on improving global pathogen surveillance

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to the project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and addressed appropriately.
