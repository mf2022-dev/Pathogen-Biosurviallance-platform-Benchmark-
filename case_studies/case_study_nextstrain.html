Case Study: Nextstrain

#1

Nextstrain

Score: 95.0 / 100 - Excellent

Nextstrain is an open-source project providing real-time tracking of pathogen evolution. It combines genomic data with geographic and temporal information to produce interactive visualizations of pathogen spread and evolution.

Key Strengths

  • Real-time phylogenetic analysis with interactive tree visualization
  • Open-source with an active global development community
  • Excellent genomic epidemiology and phylogeography tools
  • Proven in pandemic response (COVID-19, Mpox, Influenza)
  • Strong integration with GISAID and public sequence databases
